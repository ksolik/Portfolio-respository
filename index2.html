Info

I'm a UX/UI designer on a creative adventure—currently pursuing a Graphic Design degree with a side of Computer Science at the University of Wisconsin-Madison. My love for art and tech sparked my passion for crafting digital experiences that are not only visually stunning but also incredibly intuitive.

I dove into Graphic Design to unleash my creativity, and my Computer Science studies taught me how to actually build those ideas into reality. One unforgettable class blended coding with design, and that’s when it all clicked for me: why choose between art and technology when you can have the best of both worlds? I got hooked into the word of UX and UI, and now I’m all about designing user interfaces that look amazing and work flawlessly.

This portfolio isn’t just a collection of projects—it’s a playground where creativity meets code. Every animation, every responsive layout is a little piece of my heart, meticulously crafted by me. I approach design as a delightful mix of artistic flair and technical know-how, always aiming to create engaging and accessible digital experiences that truly connect with people.
